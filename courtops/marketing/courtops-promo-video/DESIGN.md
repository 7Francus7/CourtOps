# CourtOps Promo Video Design

## Style Prompt

Modern vertical SaaS promo for sports club owners: clean dark interface, precise motion, electric green accent from the CourtOps mark, and compact product mockups that feel operational and credible. The video should move quickly from paper-chaos tension into calm digital control, with sharp UI reveals, kinetic captions, and growth-focused closing energy.

## Colors

- Background: `#07100d` deep green-black
- Surface: `#101a17` graphite green card surface
- Foreground: `#f7fff9` near-white text
- Muted: `#9fb3aa` secondary copy
- Accent: `#00e676` CourtOps electric green
- Accent secondary: `#29b6f6` electric blue for data highlights
- Warning: `#ff6b6b` controlled error/problem signal

## Typography

- Primary: `Inter`, `Aptos`, `Segoe UI`, sans-serif
- Numeric and UI labels: `JetBrains Mono`, `Cascadia Mono`, monospace

## Motion

- Fast social pacing with scene beats every 1-2 seconds.
- UI panels slide and scale into place with smooth easing.
- Problem moments use jitter, red stamps, and crossed-out paper notes.
- Solution moments use clean wipes, snap-to-grid motion, and glow pulses.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use cartoon visuals or generic stock-style hero layouts.
- Do not make the whole piece a single-hue green wash.
- Do not hide the product behind abstract shapes.
- Do not use large explanatory paragraphs.
- Do not use emoji or playful sticker language.
